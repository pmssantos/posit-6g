1st Workshop on Positioning Technologies for 5G & 6G (POSIT-6G)

 

Co-located with 2024 IEEE 100th Vehicular Technology Conference (VTC2024-Fall)
7-10 October 2024, in Washington DC, USA

Scope:

Positioning remains a wide spread solutions from a variety of use cases and scenarios in 5G & 6G, such as autonomous driving, precision agriculture and asset tracking. On the last use case, as the trend towards deploying private 5G networks (e.g., in ports, production facilities, warehouses, etc.) gains traction, 5G could offer a single communication and tracking solution to serve an entire campus.

 

The first release of 5G-Advanced, in Release-18, calls for expanded and improved positioning targeting sub-10cm accuracy. This challenging goal will require researchers and developers to improve positioning technology to deal with the wide variety of scenarios that can emerge. This workshop will bring together researchers and experts from academia and industry to expose their results and views on this topic and promote the collaboration and exchange of experiences between all professionals motivated by positioning. We invite contributions in these topics.

 

Topics include but are not limited to:

- Fundamental Localization Limits

- Time-of-flight techniques and related

- Carrier phase positioning

- Positioning in 5G NR networks and beyond

- mmWave and Terahertz positioning

- AI/ML for localization and sensing

- Backscatter-based localization

- Industrial IoT applications of positioning

- Secure localization and privacy

- Phase difference techniques and related

- Sidelink and Cooperative positioning

- NLoS & in-building positioning

- Integrating positioning services in 5G/6G core

- Joint communication and positioning

- Reconfigurable intelligent surfaces
(RIS)-assisted localization and sensing

- Positioning in Non-Public 5G Networks
(ports, airports, industrial facilities)

- Vehicular and UAV applications of positioning

- Testbeds and experimentation
